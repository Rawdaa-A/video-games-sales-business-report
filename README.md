# 🎮 Video Games Sales Analysis Report

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes global video game sales data to uncover key business insights and provide actionable recommendations.

The analysis combines data exploration, visualization (Tableau), and business reporting (PowerPoint).

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
Identify key factors driving video game sales and profitability, including:
- Top-performing platforms
- Best-selling genres
- Regional market differences
- Trends over time

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🎮 Platform Performance
- Certain platforms dominate sales (e.g., PS2, Xbox)
- Platform lifecycle strongly affects revenue

### 🌍 Regional Insights
- North America leads in total sales
- Japan shows different genre preferences

### 🎯 Genre Analysis
- Action and Sports are top-selling genres
- Some niche genres generate lower but stable revenue

### 📉 Trend Analysis
- Sales peaked during specific console generations
- Decline in later years due to market saturation

---

## 📌 Conclusions
- Market performance depends heavily on platform popularity
- Regional preferences must be considered in strategy
- Genre selection significantly impacts sales success

---

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing platforms when launching games  
- Customize game genres based on region  
- Invest in trending genres like Action  
- Monitor platform lifecycle before release
